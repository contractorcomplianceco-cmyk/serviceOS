# SERVICECONNECT DISPATCH CALENDAR
# CLIENT-SPECIFIC LAYOUT REQUIREMENT

Audit and update the ServiceConnect Dispatch Calendar so it reflects the actual calendar layout and workflow requested by the client.

The attached BlueFolder calendar screenshot is a functional reference.

Do not copy BlueFolder’s outdated visual styling exactly, but preserve the workflow, density, layout logic, and ease of scanning that the client explicitly likes.

This calendar is one of the client’s most important screens.

It must not be replaced with a generic monthly calendar, appointment list, Kanban board, or simplified card grid.

==================================================
PRIMARY CALENDAR STRUCTURE
==================================================

Build a team dispatch calendar with:

- technicians listed vertically down the left side
- technician names in alphabetical order by default
- technician photo or avatar beside each name
- dates or days displayed horizontally across the top
- one horizontal row per technician
- job cards displayed inside the appropriate technician row and date column
- multiple jobs stacked vertically within the same technician/day cell
- visible empty schedule cells
- compact row heights when no jobs exist
- expanded row height when jobs are present
- horizontal scrolling for additional dates
- vertical scrolling for additional technicians
- sticky technician-name column
- sticky date/day header
- synchronized scrolling
- current-day highlight
- weekend visibility
- optional compact and expanded density modes

The screen should allow dispatchers to scan the full team schedule without opening every work order.

==================================================

==================================================

Support:

- Today
- Day
- 3-Day
- Work Week
- Full Week
- Custom Date Range
- Technician Schedule
- Region Schedule
- Unscheduled Work Drawer

The primary default should closely follow the attached screenshot:

- technicians down the left

- schedule cards inside the grid
- dense but readable operational layout

Do not make the monthly calendar the primary dispatch view.

==================================================
JOB CARD CONTENT
==================================================

Each calendar job card must show the most important details directly on the calendar.

At minimum display:

- scheduled time or time window
- internal work-order number
- external portal work-order number when applicable
- customer name
- service location or abbreviated address
- city
- source portal, such as ServiceChannel
- status
- priority
- first-trip or return-trip label
- quote status
- materials status
- important technician note
- emergency indicator
- portal-sync warning when applicable

Example structure:

7–8am  
#58470 · 348020240  
Ideal Dental – Beachwalk Shore  
ServiceChannel · DECA Dental Group  
SCHEDULED  
Priority: Urgent  
St. Johns, FL 32259  
Smoke test at 8:30am

Cards must support longer information without destroying the grid.

Use:

- concise line wrapping
- maximum visible lines
- overflow fade
- hover or click preview
- expandable detail popover
