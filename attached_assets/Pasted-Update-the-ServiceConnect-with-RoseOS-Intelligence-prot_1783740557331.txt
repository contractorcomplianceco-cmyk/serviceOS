Update the ServiceConnect with RoseOS Intelligence prototype to follow the visual design of the attached reference images.

This is a premium field-service operating system, not a generic SaaS dashboard and not a marketing site. The design should feel like a modern command center for dispatch, technicians, work orders, GPS routing, inventory, billing, and accounting.

VISUAL THESIS
ServiceConnect should feel like a high-end operations cockpit: dark command-center shell, clean white work surfaces, blue intelligence accents, polished mobile cards, sharp hierarchy, and calm professional spacing.

DESIGN DIRECTION
Match the attached references:
- Dark navy / near-black app frame
- Clean white or very light gray main work panels
- Strong electric blue accent for primary actions and intelligence highlights
- Green for completed / healthy
- Red for urgent / blocked
- Amber only for warnings, not as a brand color
- Rounded modern panels, but do not over-round everything
- Subtle shadows and depth
- Premium, app-like, operational feel
- Crisp typography
- Lots of readable spacing
- Clear status badges
- Smooth hover states
- Mobile-friendly technician screens

DO NOT:
- Do not make it look like BlueFolder.
- Do not make it look like a plain admin template.
- Do not use yellow-heavy branding.
- Do not create a marketing landing page.
- Do not use cartoon graphics.
- Do not overload the screen with random cards.
- Do not use purple SaaS gradients.
- Do not bury the working app behind hero copy.
